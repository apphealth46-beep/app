Train at ~11:30 am, then break fast at 12 pm with protein-rich meal.
Drink water, black coffee, or tea during fasting hours to stay hydrated.
Use protein powder daily to hit your protein goals (25g per scoop).
Stick to 1500 kcal/day for fat loss.
Do light cardio (walking) on rest days to boost fat loss.
Shopping list includes: eggs, chicken, fish, tuna, yogurt, veggies, fruit, rice, sweet potato, bread, avocado, almonds, olive oil.
Allow small healthy snacks (like fruit, nuts, or boiled eggs) if needed between meals to prevent overeating.
