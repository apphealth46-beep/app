3-Day Home Dumbbell + Bodyweight Workout

Home Meal Plan Tips
Day 1 – Full Body
  • Dumbbell Squat – 3x12
  • Dumbbell Bench Press – 3x10–12
  • Dumbbell Row – 3x10 each side
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 3x10
  • Plank – 3x30 sec
Day 2 – Lower Body + Core
  • Dumbbell Deadlift – 3x12
  • Lunges – 3x10 each leg
  • Dumbbell Glute Bridge – 3x12
  • Russian Twists – 3x12 each side
  • Leg Raises – 3x10
Day 3 – Upper Body
  • Bicep Curls – 3x12
  • Shoulder Press – 3x12
  • Dumbbell Row – 3x10 each side
  • Chest Fly – 3x10–12
  • Side Plank – 3x20 sec each side
